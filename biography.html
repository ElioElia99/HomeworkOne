Elio's Backstory

Elio is from a Middle Eastern country known as Lebanon, specifically the northern central village of Fouwara. Elio's mother and father are both Lebanese, and Elio has one brother and one sister. Elio loves Lebanon greatly, and always goes there for the summers and winters. However, Elio grew up in the city of Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates, where he spent 18 years of his life. He then moved to Ottawa to study at the Univsersity of Ottawa, where he is majoring in Journalism. He now lives in Ottawa with his brother. Elio enjoys creative writing, eating pizza, and listening artists like Lil Peep and Tyler the Creator.
